# SimWiki Categories

Browse our collection of affordable alternatives to commercial simulation equipment, organized by category.

## All Categories

### [Task Trainers](/categories/task-trainers)
Procedural skills trainers for practicing specific techniques

- IV cannulation arms
- Suture practice pads
- Intubation heads
- Catheterization models
- Chest tube insertion trainers
- Central line placement trainers
- Lumbar puncture models

### [Manikins & Simulators](/categories/manikins)
Full body and partial body simulation manikins

- Adult manikins
- Pediatric manikins
- Neonatal manikins
- Birthing simulators
- Trauma manikins
- High-fidelity simulators

### [Recording Equipment](/categories/recording-equipment)
Systems for recording and reviewing simulation sessions

- Network cameras
- PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom) cameras
- Microphone systems
- Recording software
- Live streaming solutions
- Debriefing room technology

### [Assessment Tools](/categories/assessment-tools)
Tools for evaluating learner performance

- Digital checklists
- Competency assessment forms
- Timing systems
- Observer tablets/devices
- Performance tracking software

### [Consumables & Supplies](/categories/consumables)
Materials used during simulation that need regular replacement

- Fake blood recipes
- Wound moulage materials
- Medication labels
- IV fluids and bags
- Disposable medical supplies
- Simulated bodily fluids

### [Monitoring Equipment](/categories/monitoring-equipment)
Vital signs and physiological monitoring displays

- ECG simulators
- Vital signs monitors
- Patient monitors
- Defibrillator trainers
- Monitor mounting solutions

### [Props & Environment](/categories/props-environment)
Creating realistic simulation environments

- Medical equipment props
- Room signage
- Storage solutions
- Furniture modifications
- Lighting solutions
- Sound effects systems

### [Technology & Software](/categories/technology)
Technical infrastructure for simulation

- Scenario control systems
- Audio/video management
- Network infrastructure
- Open-source alternatives
- Integration solutions
- Mobile apps for simulation
