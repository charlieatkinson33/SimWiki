Recording and debriefing are critical components of simulation-based learning. While commercial simulation AV systems can cost tens of thousands, effective recording and debriefing setups can be created for a fraction of the cost.

Budget Recording Solutions

Option 1: Webcam System (¬£100-150 per room)

  • Logitech C920 or C922 webcam (¬£60-80)
  • USB extension cable (¬£10)
  • Wall mount (¬£10)
  • Recording PC (shared across multiple rooms)
  • OBS Studio (free recording software)

Option 2: Action Camera System (¬£150-250 per room)

  • GoPro clone or budget action camera (¬£50-100)
  • Large capacity microSD card 128GB+ (¬£20-30)
  • External battery pack or mains adapter (¬£20-30)
  • Mounting hardware (¬£10-20)
  • Wide angle lens for full room coverage

Option 3: Smartphone Recording (¬£0-50 per room)

  • Use old smartphones as cameras (Free if available)
  • Apps like IP Webcam or DroidCam (Free)
  • Phone mounts and power adapters (¬£15-30)
  • Cloud storage or local network storage

Debriefing Setup

Basic Debriefing Room (¬£300-600)

  • Large TV or projector (¬£150-400)
  • HDMI cables and adapters (¬£20)
  • Laptop for playback (use existing)
  • Comfortable seating arrangement (use existing)
  • Whiteboard or flipchart (¬£30-50)

Enhanced Debriefing (¬£800-1,500)

  • Dual monitors or ultra-wide display (¬£400-800)
  • Annotation tablet (¬£100-200)
  • Wireless presentation system (¬£100-200)
  • Video editing software for clips (¬£100-300)

Live Observation Setup

One-Way Mirror Alternative (¬£100-300)

  • Large monitor or TV in observation room (¬£150-250)
  • HDMI extender or network video stream (¬£50-100)
  • Audio feed from simulation room
  • Comfortable seating for observers

Video Storage Solutions

Local Storage:

  • External HDD: 4-8TB (¬£100-200) - Budget option
  • NAS Device: Synology DS220+ with HDDs (¬£400-800) - Better redundancy
  • Recording PC: Multiple internal drives (¬£150-400)

Cloud Storage:

  • Google Drive: ¬£8/month for 2TB
  • OneDrive: Often included with Microsoft 365
  • Dropbox Business: ¬£12/month for 3TB
  • Consideration: Upload bandwidth and ongoing costs

Hybrid Approach:

  • Local storage for immediate playback
  • Archive to cloud for long-term storage
  • Delete local copies after set period

Video Annotation Tools

  • VLC Media Player: Free, basic playback with snapshots
  • Screencast-O-Matic: ¬£15/year, simple annotation
  • Camtasia: ¬£200 one-time, professional editing
  • Panopto: Free trial, academic pricing available
  • PowerPoint: Import video and add annotations (if you have Office)

Important Considerations

Privacy & Data Protection:

  • Obtain consent from all participants before recording
  • Secure storage with appropriate access controls
  • GDPR compliance for UK institutions
  • Clear retention and deletion policies
  • Encrypted storage for sensitive content

Technical Requirements:

  • Adequate network bandwidth for multiple streams
  • Sufficient PC processing power for multiple cameras
  • Reliable power supply with UPS backup
  • Regular backups of recordings
  • Testing before important sessions
