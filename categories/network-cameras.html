Audio Integration

Many budget IP cameras have built-in microphones, but audio quality can be poor. Consider:

  • USB Microphones: Blue Snowball or similar (¬£40-60) connected to recording PC
  • Wireless Lapel Mics: Rode Wireless GO (¬£180-200 per set) for instructor audio
  • Zoom Recorders: Zoom H1n (¬£80-100) for standalone room audio
  • Budget Option: Smartphone audio recording synchronized later (Free)

Key Considerations

  • Network Infrastructure: Ensure adequate network capacity for multiple HD streams
  • Privacy & Data Protection: Consider GDPR compliance for video storage and access
  • Mounting: Budget for camera mounts and installation (¬£10-30 per camera)
  • Lighting: Good lighting is more important than expensive cameras
  • Viewing Angles: Plan camera positions to avoid blind spots
  • Integration: ONVIF-compliant cameras can work together even from different manufacturers

Live Streaming Options

For live observation of simulation sessions:

  • Blue Iris: Built-in web interface for live viewing
  • VLC Network Streaming: Free, open RTSP streams from cameras
  • OBS Studio: Free multi-camera switching and streaming
  • Microsoft Teams/Zoom: Share camera feed via screen share for remote viewing
