# Props & Environment Setup

Creating an immersive simulation environment doesn't require expensive commercial props. With creativity and resourcefulness, you can build convincing clinical environments on a budget.

## Medical Equipment Props

### Essential Props for Clinical Realism

#### IV Poles (£10-£30 each)

**Commercial:** £100-£200

**Alternatives:**
1. **Microphone stands** - £15
   - Telescoping height
   - Wheeled base
   - Add hooks with 3D printing

2. **Camera tripods** - £20
   - Adjustable height
   - Portable
   - Add hooks/hangers

3. **Coat stands** - £10
   - Add wheels
   - Modify for IV bags
   - Paint hospital colors

**UK Suppliers:**
- B&M, Wilko (coat stands)
- Amazon UK (mic stands)
- Argos (various stands)

#### Hospital Beds

**Commercial:** £500-£2,000

**Alternatives:**
1. **Standard single beds** - £100
   - Add bed rails (£30)
   - White/clinical sheets
   - Adjustable if possible

2. **Massage tables** - £150
   - Adjustable height
   - Narrow like hospital beds
   - Portable and sturdy

3. **Camping cots** - £40
   - Cheap and portable
   - For mass casualty scenarios
   - Cover with hospital linen

**Enhancements:**
- Add side rails (DIY from PVC)
- Hospital-style sheets/covers
- Mattress protection
- Identification labels

#### Bedside Tables (£20-£50)

**Commercial:** £200-£400

**Alternatives:**
- IKEA trolleys - £25
- Small rolling carts - £20
- TV trays on wheels - £30
- Modify with paint

#### Oxygen Cylinders (Props)

**Options:**
1. **Empty cylinders** - Free
   - From medical gas companies
   - Clearly mark "TRAINING ONLY"
   - Remove/disable valves

2. **Fire extinguishers** - £20
   - Paint green for oxygen
   - Add labels
   - Clear training marking

3. **PVC pipe** - £10
   - Cut to cylinder size
   - Add top/bottom caps
   - Paint and label

**Safety:** Never use pressurized cylinders in simulation without proper training and safety measures.

#### Suction Units

**Prop Version:**
1. **Wet/dry vacuum** - £40
   - Modify appearance
   - Add tubing connection
   - Can provide actual suction

2. **Container with tubing** - £10
   - Visual prop only
   - Battery-powered pump for sound
   - Clear tubing for effect

## Room Signage and Identification

### Clinical Signage (£5-£20 per sign)

**Types Needed:**
- Room numbers/names
- Emergency exits
- Equipment labels
- Warning signs
- Directional signs
- Patient information boards

**DIY Creation:**
- Microsoft Word templates
- Canva (free account)
- Print on card/laminate
- Mount with adhesive

**Professional Look:**
- Use NHS fonts (Arial, Frutiger)
- NHS blue colors
- Standard medical symbols
- Laminate for durability

### Patient Information Boards

**Commercial:** £50-£150

**DIY Alternative:** £10-£30
- Whiteboards - £10
- Printed templates under plastic
- Label holders on wall
- Clipboard systems

**Include:**
- Patient name (scenario dependent)
- Medical warnings (allergies, etc.)
- Care instructions
- Risk assessments

## Sound Effects and Ambiance

### Creating Clinical Soundscapes

#### Essential Sounds

**Monitor Beeps:**
- Smartphone apps (free)
- Downloaded MP3s
- YouTube videos (offline download)
- Bluetooth speakers hidden - £20

**Background Hospital Noise:**
- Announcements
- Alarms in distance
- Staff conversations
- Equipment sounds

**Sources:**
- Freesound.org
- YouTube Audio Library
- BBC Sound Effects
- Record from actual hospitals (with permission)

#### Playback Systems (£30-£100)

**Options:**
1. **Bluetooth speaker** - £30
   - Hide in room
   - Control from phone
   - Good quality sound

2. **Raspberry Pi audio** - £50
   - Automated playback
   - Scheduled sounds
   - Multiple speakers

3. **Smartphone/tablet** - Existing
   - Simple and effective
   - Easy control
   - Familiar interface

### Intercom/PA System (£50-£200)

**Budget Options:**
1. **Baby monitor** - £40
   - Two-way communication
   - Acceptable quality
   - Very affordable

2. **Bluetooth speakers** - £60
   - Pair of speakers
   - Phone for announcements
   - Portable

3. **Computer speakers + mic** - £50
   - Computer in control room
   - Speakers in sim room
   - Use voice chat software
