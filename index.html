Welcome to SimWiki

Healthcare simulation equipment doesn't have to break the bank. SimWiki is your comprehensive guide to finding cost-effective alternatives to expensive industry-standard simulation equipment used in UK healthcare training.

Why SimWiki?

Healthcare simulation is essential for training medical professionals, but commercial equipment can be prohibitively expensive. SimWiki provides detailed guides on creating DIY alternatives and sourcing budget-friendly options without compromising on training quality.
