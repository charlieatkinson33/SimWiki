Welcome to SimWiki

SimWiki is a comprehensive, community-driven resource for finding cost-effective alternatives to expensive healthcare simulation equipment. Healthcare simulation is essential for training medical professionals, but commercial equipment can be prohibitively expensive. This wiki provides detailed guides on creating DIY alternatives and sourcing budget-friendly options without compromising on training quality.

Created for UK simulation educators, SimWiki shares "McGyvering" techniques and creative solutions that can save thousands of pounds while maintaining educational effectiveness.

Why SimWiki?

Healthcare simulation equipment doesn't have to break the bank. This collaborative platform allows simulation educators to share:

  • DIY alternatives to commercial equipment
  • Step-by-step construction guides with detailed instructions
  • Cost comparisons and savings - see how much you can save
  • UK-specific supplier information for easy sourcing
  • Practical, tested solutions from the community

About This Wiki

SimWiki is focused on the UK healthcare simulation context, but the principles and techniques can be adapted globally. We prioritize:

  • Educational effectiveness - alternatives must meet learning objectives
  • Safety - all solutions are vetted for participant safety
  • Cost-effectiveness - significant savings over commercial options
  • Sustainability - environmentally conscious solutions where possible
  • Community collaboration - shared knowledge benefits everyone
