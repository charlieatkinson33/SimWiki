## üöÄ Creating Your Account

### Quick Start (2 minutes)

1. **Go to GitHub**
   - Visit [github.com/signup](https://github.com/signup)

2. **Enter your details**
   - Email address
   - Password (strong and unique)
   - Username (choose wisely - it's your identity)
   - Verify you're human

3. **Verify your email**
   - Check your inbox
   - Click the verification link
   - **Done!**

### Choosing a Username

Your username will be visible on all your contributions. Consider:

‚úÖ **Good choices:**
- Your real name (john-smith)
- Professional handle (dr-jones-med)
- Anonymous but professional (simulation-educator-uk)

‚ùå **Avoid:**
- Unprofessional names
- Names you might regret
- Temporary or joke names

üí° **Tip:** Your username can be changed later, but it's better to start with a good one.

### Best Practices

## üîí Privacy & Visibility

### What's Public

By default, these are public:
- Your contributions to SimWiki
- Your username
- Your profile information (what you choose)
- Your commit history
- Your comments and discussions

### What's Private

These remain private:
- Your email address (unless you make it public)
- Your password
- Your two-factor authentication
- Private repositories (not applicable to SimWiki)
- Draft pull requests (if you choose)

### Privacy Controls

Manage your privacy:

1. **Email privacy:**
   - Settings ‚Üí Emails
   - Check "Keep my email addresses private"
   - GitHub will use noreply email in commits

2. **Profile visibility:**
   - Control what appears on profile
   - Hide private contributions
   - Make profile private (but contributions stay public)

3. **Activity visibility:**
   - Control who sees your activity
   - Manage notification settings

## üìß Notification Management

### Email Notifications

Control what you receive:

**Settings ‚Üí Notifications:**

### Notification Types

- **Participating:** Changes to discussions you're involved in
- **Watching:** Repository you're watching
- **@Mentions:** When someone tags you
- **Pull request reviews:** When assigned as reviewer
- **Issue assignments:** When assigned an issue

### Reducing Email

Too many emails? Try:
- **Custom watching:** Only specific events
- **Web notifications only:** Check on GitHub
- **Weekly digests:** One summary email
- **Unwatch:** Stop watching specific items

## üÜò Account Help

### Common Issues

**"I forgot my password"**
- Go to [password reset](https://github.com/password_reset)
- Enter your email
- Follow reset instructions
- Consider using a password manager

**"I can't access my account"**
- Check if 2FA is the issue
- Use recovery codes if you have them
- Contact GitHub support
- Create new account as last resort

**"I want to change my username"**
- Settings ‚Üí Account
- Change username (rarely needed)
- Redirects set up automatically
- Previous contributions still count

**"My email isn't verified"**
- Check spam folder
- Request new verification email
- Make sure email is correct
- Try different email

### Getting Support

**Account issues:**
- [GitHub Support](https://support.github.com/)
- [GitHub Community](https://github.community/)
- GitHub's documentation

**SimWiki-specific:**
- [SimWiki Discussions](https://github.com/charlieatkinson33/SimWiki/discussions)
